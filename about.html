This festival represents the coming together of Parvatibai Genba Moze College’s vibrant cultural and technical traditions. By uniting these diverse events under one banner, Technomeet honors the college’s rich heritage while fostering a forward-looking vision that encourages collaboration, cultural enrichment, and technological advancement.

About Parvatibai Genba Moze College

Parvatibai Genba Moze College of Engineering (PGMCOE), Wagholi, Pune, is a private technical institution established in 2006 under the auspices of the Genba Sopanrao Moze Trust, founded by Shri Rambhau Moze. The college is affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), with accreditation from the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). Located on a sprawling campus in Wagholi, the college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, information technology, and management, combining theoretical learning with practical exposure. According to aggregated reviews from students and users, PGMCOE has an overall rating of 4.5 out of 5, with positive feedback on faculty support, infrastructure, and placement assistance, while also noting areas for continued development in certain facilities and resources.
