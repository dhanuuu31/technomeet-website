Event Highlights

  • Open to individual participants or teams (max 4 members).
  • Both working and non-working models are allowed.
  • Models must demonstrate innovation, practical relevance, and creativity.
  • Judging based on innovation, explanation, relevance, and presentation.
  • A great opportunity for exposure, learning, and recognition.

Rules & Regulations

  • Only registered participants are allowed to participate in the Project Expo.
  • Participation can be individual or team-based, as per registration details.
  • All participants must carry a valid college ID card on the event day.
  • The project presented must be original; copied or plagiarized work is strictly prohibited.
  • A ready and properly working project is required for evaluation.
  • The time limit for project presentation will be fixed and must be strictly followed.
  • One team member must present and explain the project to the judges.
  • Use of any unfair means during the event is strictly prohibited.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding on all participants.

Preparation Tips

  • Focus on a clear problem statement and objective for your model.
  • Prepare a working prototype or demo if possible.
  • Create a presentation highlighting idea, innovation, and real-world application.
  • Practice explaining the model concisely to judges and attendees.
  • Ensure all components and visuals are ready and functional.
